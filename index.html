Marvellous Manouvering Meerkat

The meeting

My wife and I visited the zoo one day and a random meerkat approached us and wanted to come along. She told us that her name is Marvellous Manouvering Meerkat .

MMM had a wish and it was to explore the web. So she came with us and wanted me to upload her, so here she is.

MMM would like to join you too on your journey on the web, so she would be able to explore as much as possible.

She can join you in two ways:
  • Through a browser extension
  • With a JavaScript code

Browser Extension

  1. Open the extension page.
  2. Click 'Add to Chrome'. (Also works in Edge, Opera, Brave, Vivaldi, etc.)
  3. Click the 'Add extension' button.
  4. Open any* website and MMM will be there with you.

JavaScript Code

  1. Open the source of a website.
  2. Copy the following code to the head section of the code. 
    <script src=
"https://marvellous-manouvering-meerkat.harka.com/mmm.js"
defer>
</script>
  3. Save/deploy the site.
  4. Now anybody who visits your website can meet MMM.

* MMM is very persistent, however there are a very few websites, which block her from joining you. Don't worry if you can't see her on one site, she will surely join you on the next one.
